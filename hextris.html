HEXTRIS

Master the hexagonal twist on a classic puzzle challenge

ABOUT THE GAME

Hextris reimagines the classic block-falling puzzle game with an innovative hexagonal playfield. Position and rotate falling pieces to create complete lines and prevent the board from filling up. With its hypnotic visuals, electronic soundtrack, and progressively challenging gameplay, Hextris offers a fresh perspective on puzzle gaming that will keep you engaged for hours.

The game's deceptively simple mechanics hide layers of strategic depth, challenging players to think in new geometric patterns. The hexagonal structure creates unique opportunities for chain reactions and complex piece arrangements that aren't possible in traditional four-sided puzzle games. As you progress, the increasing speed and complex piece formations test both your reflexes and spatial reasoning.

GAMEPLAY

In Hextris, blocks fall from the six sides of the hexagon toward the center. Your task is to rotate the hexagon to position the falling blocks and create complete lines. When you complete a line, it disappears, making room for new blocks. The game ends when blocks stack up to the edge of the playing field. The longer you play, the faster the blocks fall, increasing the challenge. Your score rises with each block placed and line cleared, with bonus points for combo moves.

TIPS & HINTS

  • Plan ahead by watching the next block indicator to strategize your moves.
  • Focus on keeping the central area clear to give yourself more options.
  • Try to create multiple line clears with a single block placement for maximum points.
  • When the game speeds up, prioritize stability over complex strategies.
